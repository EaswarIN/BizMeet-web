KEEP IN TOUCH WITH YOUR NEARBY CONTACTS

Set up ad hoc meetings with one or more contacts while on the move. Have you been on business trips attending a show or event and thought maybe so & so are attending as well? Simply check your map and if a contact is nearby, message him or her for a quick meeting.

YOUR BIZMEET CONTACT BOOK IS YOUR UNIVERSE

You choose which of your contacts on BizMeet you want to follow and what their proximity to your location should be. If you don't see friends, family or colleagues on BizMeet? Simply invite them directly from the BizMeet Invite menu and expand your universe.

ALWAYS IN CONTROL OF PRIVACY & VISIBILITY

BizMeet puts you in control in regard to who can see you. You can turn off your visibility to others universally, or only for individual users, depending on your personal preferences.

SEAMLESS INTEGRATION WITH YOUR CONTACT DATA

BizMeet automatically syncs with your mobile's contact list, so you know which of your contacts are on BizMeet and which are not.

EASILY COMMUNICATE WITH YOUR BIZMEET CONTACTS

Found a client or friend nearby that you want to meet up with? Tap their icon on the map and send them a message or a meeting invite.

SECURITY KEPT AT THE FOREFRONT

BizMeet users cannot follow, chat or set up meetings with each other over BizMeet, unless they are mutually connected through their phones mobile contact lists. Ensuring you that only those contacts you want to be followed by can do so.
