BizMeet Privacy Policy

v3 August 26th, 2015

BizMeet ("HubLink Networks AG, hereafter HLN") takes your privacy seriously. We are as such committed to give you transparency of our privacy practices and control over your data in connection with BizMeet Services. Please read the following to learn more about our Privacy Policy ("Policy"). This Policy details how we receive, collect and use information in connection with our BizMeet software ("BizMeet App"), the BizMeet-hub.com website ("Site") and other products and services ("Service") provided through the BizMeet app or HLN.

By using BizMeet, you are accepting the policies and practices described in this Policy, as such the Policy may be amended from time to time. Each time you use BizMeet, you agree and expressly consent to our collection, use and disclosure of the information that you provide as described in this Policy.

This Privacy Policy covers HLN treatment of personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") and other data that HubLink Networks gathers when you are accessing BizMeet's Services.

1. Collection and Use of Information

BizMeet App

When you install and register the BizMeet app, you will be asked to provide us with your phone number, name, email address, password and a photo (name and photo are not mandatory) and to allow us access to your mobile device's address book.

Only a copy of the phone numbers of your address book (but not names, emails, notes or any other Personal Information from your contacts in your address book) will be stored on our servers and will only be used to:

(a) Indicate which of your contacts are already BizMeet users and mutually connected with you.

(b) Which are BizMeet users, but not mutually connected and which are not on BizMeet.

(c) Indicate which of your contacts are nearby.

(d) Sync your contacts with the BizMeet server to maintain accurate and up to date information in alignment with our user safety policy.

The copy of your contacts phone numbers is stored on a live database. If you request to deactivate your account (more about this below under Section 7), it will be deleted instantly and permanently.

Your phone number is used for identification purposes as your BizMeet user ID and is stored in our database, and will be considered private information by HLN and therefore not distributed or shared with third parties for any reason. However, since other BizMeet users may add your BizMeet number to their address book to follow you or see if you are registered with BizMeet, we do not consider the fact that your BizMeet number exists on BizMeet for the purpose of connecting with other BizMeet users as private information.

BizMeet may interact with your phone's native messaging app for the sole purpose of;

(a) Provide registration verification number for activating the BizMeet service and

(b) enabling you the ability to invite potential users to BizMeet through SMS.

WE WILL NOT ACCESS THE CONTENTS OF ANY OTHER SMS MESSAGES. We use the services of third parties to process your registration verification number and we require that these third parties take the appropriate organizational and technical measures to protect your personal data and traffic data and to comply with relevant laws.

With location services being at the core of the BizMeet App we automatically receive your location when you use the Services. We use various technologies to determine location, such as location services of the applicable operating system or browser and sensor data from your device that may, for example, provide information on nearby Wi-Fi or Beacon access points and cell towers.

Name and Photo

Should you provide your name and/or photo it will be visible to other BizMeet users as long as such users have your BizMeet number.

Online, Messaging and Meeting Status

To ensure our user privacy standards are met, there are no specific indicators displayed as to whether a user is online or not, at any given time. Other users that are able to see and follow you as a result of your connection status and privacy settings (Hide/Unhide) may be able to determine your status by observing your movement across the map.

At this time BizMeet does not provide "Delivered" or "Seen" status indications for chat messages sent between users. Meeting status is only indicated with "Sent" or "Accepted" status, declined or canceled meeting statuses are not displayed.

Location Information

BizMeet allows you to share your location with other users with whom you are connected through your address book. If you choose to share your location with a specific user or group of users your location will be retrieved from your device and sent to BizMeet. BizMeet will then in turn only send the location information to the devices of users that are following you, and from which you have not hidden yourself. BizMeet only relays the location data among consenting users and will not share or store your location information and do not use it for any purpose except for the inherent purpose of the app as specifically utilized by you.

BizMeet Invite

If you wish to use BizMeet to invite contacts in your address book outside of the BizMeet community ("BizMeet Invite"), you will be sending an SMS through your native messaging app on your device. BizMeet and its parent HLN does not charge you the user for any such messaging, however, please be advised that based on your mobile contract with your carrier, your carrier may charge you for such SMS.

Cookies, Clear GIFs and Other Automatically Collected Data

If you have permitted your computer to accept cookies, we may use cookies on the Site. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to an individual's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies make web-surfing easier by saving preferences and browsing patterns while you are at the Site. Cookies may be used to analyze the Site usage, improve content, customize the Site's content and layout and for other customer service purposes. A cookie may enable us to relate your use of our Site to other information about you, including your Personal Information. All of these purposes serve to improve and personalize your experience on our Site. Most Web browsers can be set to inform you when a cookie has been sent to you and provide you with the opportunity to refuse that cookie. Additionally, if you have a Flash player installed on your computer, your Flash player can be set to reject or delete Flash cookies. However, refusing a cookie may, in some cases, preclude you from using, or negatively impact the display or function of, the Site or certain areas or features of the Site.

We may use "clear GIFs" (aka "Web beacons" or "pixel tags") or similar technologies, in the Site and/or in our communications with you to enable us to evaluate Site usage information about visitors to the Site, target campaigns, upgrade visitor information, and know whether you have visited a Web page or received a message. A clear GIF is typically a one-pixel, transparent image (although it can be a visible image as well), located on a Web page or in an e-mail or other type of message, which is retrieved from a remote site on the Internet enabling the verification of an individual's viewing or receipt of a Web page or message. A clear gif may enable us to relate your viewing or receipt of a Web page or message to other information about you, including your Personal Information.

Our servers may automatically collect data about your Internet address when you visit the Site. This information, known as an Internet Protocol address, or IP Address, is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer by your Internet service provider whenever you are on the Internet. When you request pages from our Site, our servers may log your IP Address and, if applicable, your domain name. Your IP Address is used to help identify you and to gather demographic information about our members and users as a whole, but does not include personally identifiable information. Our server may also record the referring page that linked you to us (e.g., another web site or a search engine); the pages you visit on this Site; the web site you visit after this Site; the ads you see and/or click on; other information about the type of Web browser, computer, platform, related software and settings you are using; any search terms you have entered on this Site or a referral site; and other Web usage activity and data logged by our servers. We use this information for internal system administration, to help diagnose problems with our servers, and to administer our Service. Such information may also be used to gather demographic information, such as country of origin and Internet Service Provider.

We will not link any Personal Information, including email addresses, with aggregate data of our users.

Any or all of these activities with regard to Site usage information may be performed on our behalf by our service providers, including, for example, our development partner(s), our analytics vendor(s) and our SMS/email management partner(s).

BizMeet may use Google Analytics to help us anonymously track and report user/visitor behavior information and users' standard log information to the Site and the BizMeet App. This information (including but not limited to: IP address, data storage, maintenance services, database management, web analytics and information processing) helps us analyze and evaluate how the BizMeet App and Site are used as part of our ongoing efforts to improve the Site and the BizMeet App's features and services. No personally identifying data is included in this type of reporting. Google Analytics may have access to your information only for the purposes of performing these tasks and on behalf of HLN and under obligation similar in those in this Privacy Policy. For more information about the terms that govern Google Analytics, please visit the Google Analytics terms of service.

We will never (and will not allow any third party to) use the statistical analytics tool to track or to collect any Personal Information of visitors or users. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. Neither we nor Google will link, or seek to link, an IP address with the identity of a device user. 7. Change or Removal of Information

If you provided your email address to us and wish to update it, you may do so by sending an email to support@bizmeet-hub.com.

If you no longer wish to receive e-mail or message communication from us, such as offers, update information and newsletters, you may opt-out of receiving such communications by going to OPT-OUT under settings in the BizMeet app and change the slider setting from off to on.

Due to technological reasons, we do not know when and if you delete the application. Therefore, if we detect no activity from your device for 4 hours we will dim your location indicator on the map and completely remove it after 24 hours. Furthermore, if there has been no activity for 90 days, we will assume that you deleted the application. However, since we are not certain that you deleted the application (you may just be on an extended vacation, etc.) we will not deactivate your BizMeet account (which is your phone number and device ID). We do not want to force you to register again in the event of non-use.

If you wish to delete your account completely, you can do so at any time by going to DEACTIVATE ACCOUNT under settings and enter the information needed to deactivate your account. Please note that this will remove your data from BizMeet, and deactivate BizMeet on your device. Because of the way we maintain BizMeet, such deletion may not be immediate, and residual copies of your profile information may remain on backup media for up to ninety (90) days. If you have additional concerns and/or requests, you can contact BizMeet at support@bizmeet-hub.com.
